About Company

WE ARE MAK REMOVALS

"MAK REMOVAL AND STORAGE INDIA PVT LTD" AN ISO 9001:2015 CERTIFIED MAK Removal is a leading Packing and Moving Company well known for Relocation Services such as Household Goods (Domestics and International), Storage , Fine Arts, Vehicle Shifting , Corporate Moving and Pet Moving Services.

Mak Removals is a renowned name in the RELOCATION Industry run by a professional with 2 Decades of experience. MAK Group is present at four locations in India with its Branch and 25 associates’ partners at strategic locations serving 100 no of cities countrywide.

MAK has established quality services, resources, and global network service partners. All dedicated to ensuring each relocation as stress-free as possible for customers.

Our On-time response and regular status assure our customer’s, a move to their New home as planned without extra cost. We as a group have International certifications and Network partners worldwide to execute smooth and hassle-free Domestic & International Moves.
